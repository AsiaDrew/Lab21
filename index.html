Miles Morales
SPIDER-MAN

Emerging from a universe in need of help, a Brooklyn teen named Miles Morales rose to the challenge. Reluctant at first, he quickly earned the mantle of a mega Super Hero. Gifted with cool web-shooters and guided by Peter Parker him-self, Miles Morales uses his abilities to be a different kind of Spider-Man.

POWERS AND ABILITIES
SUPERHUMAN ENDURANCE

Much like Peter Parker, Morales possesses the proportionate strength, speed, stamina, durability, and reflexes of a spider.

BIO-ELECTROKENISIS

In addition to a Spider-Sense, Miles also has the power to generate bio-electrokenisis powers, which he uses to stun his enemies or engage camoflauge.
